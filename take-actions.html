Get Involved: Main Programs

Charity: Water offers several active and engaging programs. You can join and make a difference!

  • Fundraising
    Start your own fundraising campaign to help bring clean water to communities in need.
  • Water Project
    Support or learn more about our water projects around the world.
  • Brand Partner
    Become a brand partner and help us reach more people together.
  • Legacy Giving
    Leave a lasting impact by including Charity: Water in your will or estate plan.
  • Experiment Lab Visitation
    Visit our Experiment Lab to see innovation in action and learn how we develop new solutions.
